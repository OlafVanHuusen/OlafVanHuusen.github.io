Welcome to Word Survival

Can you survive when every second counts? You have just one minute to stay in the game!

Form words by selecting adjacent letters in the grid. Each valid word you find adds precious seconds to your timer:

  • +10 seconds for every word
  • +5 additional seconds for each letter beyond 3

Words must be at least 3 letters long. Letters are replaced once used, so plan carefully!

How long can you survive? Challenge yourself to beat your highest score and longest survival time!
